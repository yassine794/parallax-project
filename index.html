NEON CITY

Cyberpunk Parallax Experience

Welcome to 2099

Every district tells a different story. The upper sectors shine with luxury, chrome, and endless digital entertainment, while the lower levels pulse with black markets, cybernetic enhancements, and forgotten people surviving in the shadows.

As you scroll deeper into the city, the layered parallax environment reveals a futuristic world inspired by cyberpunk cinema, neon-lit architecture, and dystopian sci-fi atmospheres.

Somewhere beyond the endless neon haze, the city continues to expand — alive with electric dreams, artificial stars, and whispers carried through forgotten networks. Every light hides a story, every shadow conceals another secret waiting beneath the chrome skyline.
